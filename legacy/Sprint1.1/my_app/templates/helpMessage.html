Poker score to evaluate a task

The Poker score is a technique to be able to determin the difficulty of a task through a team consensus.

During the start of a sprint the team will get together and evaluate the tasks given with the fibonacci sequence.

Everyone picks a score and they are revealed once everyone has made their choice.

Based upon this everyones results are discussed and the process is repeated until a concensus is reached.
