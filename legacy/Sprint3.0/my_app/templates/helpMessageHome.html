Poker score to evaluate a task

The Poker score is a technique to be able to determin the difficulty of a task through a team consensus.

During the start of a sprint the team will get together and evaluate the tasks given with the fibonacci sequence.

Everyone picks a score and they are revealed once everyone has made their choice.

Based upon this everyones results are discussed and the process is repeated until a concensus is reached.

What is a Sprint?

A Sprint is a short timespan ranging from weeks to a couple months at most usually.

During this timeframe the team completes a set amount of work determined by picking and evaluating user stories.

Furthermore during this timespan the team meets regularly for scrums, usually every other day, in order to discuss the progress.

Once the sprint is over the team will have a meeting to discuss what went well and what could be improved.
